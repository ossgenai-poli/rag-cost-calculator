A

Two color systems you'll see everywhere

Every input carries a taxonomy chip (who supplies it). Every fleet recommendation carries a confidence chip (how good the evidence is). They are visually distinct on purpose: taxonomy is outlined, confidence is filled.

Field taxonomy — who supplies it

FactThe SA enters it — a value a customer plausibly knows.
DerivedCalculated from other inputs — read-only unless Expert.
RecommendedApp default, chosen with a reason — editable.
ExpertHidden until you open a Tune drawer.

Confidence ladder — beside the fleet

MeasuredExact model, GPU, precision, sequence, topology, traceable run.
Measured·scaledExact serving group; input throughput scaled to your ISL.
ExtrapolatedSequence / precision / topology differs.
ProxyAnother model's benchmark used as a stand-in.
HeuristicNo applicable benchmark — a rough estimate.
